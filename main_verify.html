Welcome to VerifAI

Our artificial intelligence tool will analyze your content detecting common patterns among thousands of audited misinformation news.

Its detection algorithm is based on four key vectors: disinformation, source authority, sensationalism and context. The probability of matching one each of them assigns the content a score, out of 100 points, showing the potential intention of deception.

The results are shared with trusted media to ease real-time fact-checking tasks, as well as with search engines and social media, keeping the content open and free to everyone.
